Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my calorie and protein goals?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and enter your daily calorie and protein targets. You can update these at any time.

How do I log a meal?

From the Dashboard, tap + on a meal section. You can search the food database, describe food in natural language, enter nutrition manually, or build a custom meal from ingredients.

Do I need an account?

Yes. You need to sign up with an email and password to use Irada. This allows your data to be securely stored and synced across your devices.

Where is my data stored?

Your nutrition data is securely stored in the cloud using your account. This means your data is backed up and accessible whenever you sign in.

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Settings and use the Export Data option to download a backup file of all your entries.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings and tap Clear All Data. This permanently removes all your entries. To delete your account entirely, contact us via email.

Privacy

Irada respects your privacy. Your data is securely stored using your account and is not shared with third parties. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
