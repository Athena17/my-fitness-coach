Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my calorie and protein goals?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and enter your daily calorie and protein targets. You can update these at any time.

How do I log a meal?

From the Dashboard, tap + on a meal section. You can search the food database, describe food in natural language, enter nutrition manually, or build a custom meal from ingredients.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Settings and use the Export Data option to download a backup file of all your entries.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings and tap Clear All Data. This permanently removes all entries from your device.

Privacy

Irada respects your privacy. All nutrition data stays on your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising. No account or sign-up is required.
